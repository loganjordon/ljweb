ABOUT ME

Logan Jordon is a software developer for Field Agent in Fayetteville, Arkansas. He graduated from the University of Arkansas and earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. His hobbies include reading, writing, and cooking breakfast almost every morning. He's been married to his beautiful wife, Sierra, since May of 2019.

Logan is also the dungeon master for his family's D&D campaign. The party -- consisting of a half-elf bard, a legendary dragonborn pirate, a grizzled human ranger, and a dragonborn monk master -- regularly traverse the continent of Trondheim on the weekends.

Logan is a Christian, and regularly attends Cross Church. One of his favorite verses in the Bible is Galatians 1:10.

"For am I now seeking the approval of man, or of God? Or am I trying to please man? If I were still trying to please man, I would not be a servant of Christ." Galatians 1:10 (ESV)
